## 🎯 PRESENTATION TALKING POINTS

### Opening (30 seconds)
> "This is Kushukushu ERP - a complete warehouse management system built specifically for wheat flour factories. It manages sales, inventory, production, and finance across multiple branches with real-time visibility and multi-level approval controls."

### Dashboard (1 minute)
> "The Owner Control Room gives you a complete view of your business in real-time. You can see sales, production, approvals, and staff at a glance. Compare branches side-by-side, and watch live activity as it happens across all operations. Everything auto-refreshes every 30 seconds."

### POS & Loans (2 minutes)
> "The Point of Sale is branch-specific - sales reps only see products available at their branch. When a customer needs credit, the system automatically creates a customer record and loan with a 30-day term. Finance gets automatically notified, inventory is deducted, and the customer's credit is tracked. All automatic."

### Workflows (2 minutes)
> "Stock requests go through 6 stages: Sales creates, Admin approves and reserves inventory, Manager verifies capacity, Storekeeper fulfills and deducts stock, Guard releases with gate pass, and Sales confirms receipt. You can't skip stages, and every action is logged with who did it and when."

### Production (1.5 minutes)
> "Managers record wheat deliveries with quality ratings. When milling completes, they log ALL outputs - finished products AND by-products - in a single screen. Everything automatically goes into inventory. The system calculates recovery rates and provides complete traceability from wheat to customer."

### Finance (1.5 minutes)
> "Finance sees every transaction automatically created from sales, loan payments, and purchases. Spending limits are enforced - large purchases need Owner authorization. The system routes transactions to the right accounts automatically: cash to cash account, checks to bank, loans to accounts receivable."

### Closing (30 seconds)
> "This is production-ready code. Core operations work today. The remaining work is advanced reports and charts. You can deploy and start using this now, and we'll add the polish while you operate."
